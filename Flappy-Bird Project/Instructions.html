Instructions

Launch the game: Open the game on your device.

Start flying: Tap the screen to make your bird flap its wings and soar.

Release to drop.

Pipe Navigation: Maneuver your bird through the gaps in the pipes.

This requires precise timing and a steady hand to avoid collisions.

Continue like this: The game continues until your bird meets the pipe more than twice,

your goal is to get the highest possible score by passing through as many pipes as possible.

Challenge Friends: Compete with friends by comparing scores.

The top five scores can be seen in the highlights.

Good luck and have fun!
